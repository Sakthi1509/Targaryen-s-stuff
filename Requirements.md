# BharatPath AI - Requirements Document

## 1. Overview

BharatPath AI is an AI-powered platform that helps Indian students and job aspirants stay updated with government exams, recruitment notifications, and welfare schemes. It provides personalized recommendations, AI-driven explanations, smart study assistance, and real-time push notifications.

The goal is to simplify access to verified government information and improve learning & preparation productivity.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Millions of Indian students miss opportunities due to:

- Scattered and unorganized government notifications
- Fake job alerts and misinformation
- Difficulty understanding eligibility criteria
- Lack of personalized exam/scheme suggestions
- No structured AI-based study support

There is no centralized AI system that filters, verifies, personalizes, and explains opportunities intelligently.

---

## 3. Objectives

- Aggregate official government exam and scheme notifications daily
- Personalize recommendations based on user profile
- Provide AI-powered eligibility analysis
- Offer AI-based study assistant (notes, sums, mock tests)
- Send smart push notifications
- Support regional language explanations

---

## 4. Target Users

- Government exam aspirants (SSC, UPSC, TNPSC, Railways, Banking, Defence, etc.)
- Rural and Tier-2/Tier-3 students
- First-generation graduates
- Scholarship seekers
- Defence (Agnipath) aspirants

---

## 5. Functional Requirements

### 5.1 User Profile Management
- User registration and login
- Profile inputs: age, education, category, location, interests
- Store exam preferences

### 5.2 Notification Aggregation Engine
- Fetch official updates from verified sources
- Daily automated update pipeline
- Store announcements in structured format

### 5.3 AI Personalization Engine
- Match user profile with eligibility criteria
- Rank relevance score
- Suggest high-priority opportunities

### 5.4 AI Chat Assistant
- Answer user queries about exams/schemes
- Explain eligibility criteria in simple language
- Translate explanations into regional languages

### 5.5 Study Assistant
- Generate notes
- Solve maths problems step-by-step
- Generate quizzes
- Mock test generator
- Weak-area detection

### 5.6 Smart Notifications
- Push alerts for deadlines
- Personalized reminders
- Daily important announcement summary

---

## 6. Non-Functional Requirements

- Scalable to millions of users
- Low latency AI responses
- Secure user data handling
- Verified source validation
- High availability (99% uptime)
- Multi-language support

---

## 7. Constraints

- Limited initial data sources
- Dependence on AI model accuracy
- Internet connectivity for updates

---

## 8. Success Metrics

- User engagement rate
- Daily active users
- Notification relevance accuracy
- Improvement in mock test scores
- User satisfaction rating
